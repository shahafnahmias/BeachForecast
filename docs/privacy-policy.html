Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Beach Forecast ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

The App accesses your device's location (GPS) solely to determine the nearest beach and provide relevant marine weather data.

How We Use Your Data

  • Location data is used only to fetch weather forecasts for the closest coastal area.
  • Your preferences (units, theme, language, selected beach) are stored locally on your device.

Background Location Access

Our home screen widgets periodically access your last known location (approximately every 15 minutes) to update weather conditions, even when the app is not open. We use your last known location (not continuous tracking) to minimize battery impact. Location data is:

  • Never stored on remote servers
  • Only used to fetch weather data for your selected beach
  • Cached locally for 24 hours to reduce GPS usage

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit personal information to any server. Weather data is fetched from the Open-Meteo Marine API using your coordinates; no identifying information is sent.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any personal data to third parties.

Advertising & Analytics

The App contains no ads. We use Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous crash reports and usage statistics (e.g., app opens, screen views). No personally identifiable information is collected through these services. You may disable analytics collection at any time in the Settings screen.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

How to Delete Your Data

All your data is stored locally on your device:

  • To delete your preferences: Go to Android Settings > Apps > Beach Forecast > Storage > Clear Data
  • To delete location cache: Simply uninstall the app
  • We do not store any data on remote servers, so there is nothing to delete remotely

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the App.
